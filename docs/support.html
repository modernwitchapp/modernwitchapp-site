Support

Need help with Modern Witch Digital Spellbook? Email us and we’ll get back to you as soon as possible.

Contact

  • Support email: support@modernwitchapp.com

Include this in your message

  • What you were trying to do when the issue happened
  • Your device model (example: iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Any screenshots (if relevant)

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. If you’re having trouble with access after subscribing, include the email used for your Apple ID (if you’re comfortable) and the date/time of the purchase. Do not send payment card details.

Troubleshooting

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Confirm you’re on the latest version of iOS
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app (only if your content is synced to your account)

If you’re reporting a bug, a short “steps to reproduce” list is incredibly helpful.
