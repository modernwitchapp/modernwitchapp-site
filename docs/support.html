Support

For help with Modern Witch Digital Spellbook, contact us using the email below.

Contact

  • Support email: support@modernwitchapp.com

Support messages are reviewed and responded to within 2 business days.

Include this in your message

  • What you were doing when the issue occurred
  • Your device model (example: iPhone 14 Pro)
  • Your iOS version (Settings → General → About)
  • Screenshots, if relevant

Subscriptions

Subscriptions are managed by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

If you are experiencing access issues after subscribing, include:

  • The email address associated with your Apple ID
  • The approximate date and time of purchase

Do not send payment card details.

Troubleshooting

  • Force close the app and reopen it
  • Confirm you are running the latest version of iOS
  • Restart your device
  • Reinstall the app only if your content is synced to your account

When reporting a bug, a short list of steps to reproduce the issue is helpful.
